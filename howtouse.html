How to Use

MINES PREDICTER

  • Select your mines using the dropdown arrow.
  • Click START to reveal results.
  • If your 1st bet loss then follow same prediction with 2nd level then 3rd and 4th.
  • Use NEXT to continue playing with the same mines.
  • To change mines, click the arrow again → it resets everything.
  • Never bet all wallet always play with level and suffisent balance.
